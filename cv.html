Working as a developer within uSwitch's Advice and Innovations team, I have been responsible for collaboratively devising, creating and testing new business ideas; maintaining and improving existing personal finance offerings; and creating a new application for the car insurance comparison journey.

Working on a small, self-organizing team of 4 developers, I have had the chance to develop software using Rails, Sinatra, node.js and Clojure. I maintain a focus on back-end development while assisting our frontend developer with his work and taking on frontend tasks myself when there is excess demand. I also share in the responsibility for maintaining and configuring our operations platform, using Puppet and AWS extensively.

I am frequently involved in conversations regarding the management of workflow and allocation of development efforts, aiming to achieve consensus from all team members on what are the priorities and constraints, based both on financial metrics and on our long-term goals for the team and the business.

Whilst working at Forward I have continued to speak at conferences and user groups, including Ruby Manor, the London Ruby User Group, and internal development meetings and showcases.
