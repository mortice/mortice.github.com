see something good
twist
turn
something good

a toy, a tool, a tube
a navigator's aid
a guide

you were inside
or i thought you were
or we both were
then that moment, tangible
refracted and distant
went with a turn of the wrist
a look in the eye
a turn of phrase
a look away

can i plot my way?
by the stars, the shapes, the signs
that we build inside?

see something good
a new way
an old way
a tool, a toy
